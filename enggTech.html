Engineering & Technology Writing from qualified Expert

Engineering research focuses on discovering and systematically organizing knowledge. Engineers design, develop, construct, and operate systems, machines, and devices of societal and economic value. The importance of an application determines the focus of engineering research. Unlike science, which explores natural phenomena, engineering integrates experience, methods, techniques, and knowledge from various fields, considering adaptability, producibility, computability, and costs. It relies on empirical, experimental, and heuristic methods grounded in scientific principles, with constant support from mentors.
